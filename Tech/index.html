TitleMaat

This is a story about “self” and “fear”, human is a sociable animal, which means we cannot avoid interacting with external environment. So, many of us would be easily affected by others. So, in this game, while in the process of avoiding enemies and then collecting clues, "I" realized that these monsters might not hurt me but were just a projection of my own fears and anxieties.
